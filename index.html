Getting Started: OpenRCT2 Online Tutorial

New to OpenRCT2? This walkthrough gets you from an empty park to your first profitable rides. For deeper learning, visit the OpenRCT2 site.

1) Start a Park & Pause

  • Begin with New Game (scenario) or Sandbox.
  • Pause time while planning paths, entrances, and ride placement.
  • Use the camera controls to scout flat land and key areas.

2) Lay Down Paths & Essentials

  • Build paths from the park entrance toward your first ride area.
  • Add benches and bins early to keep guests happy.
  • Place bathrooms and a few lamps near busy routes.

3) First Ride: Simple Profit

  • Place a gentle ride (carousel / ferris wheel) near the entrance.
  • Build the queue + exit and connect them with paths.
  • Set a reasonable ticket price and open the ride.
  • Watch guest thoughts to tune price and capacity.

4) Staff & Cleanliness

  • Hire handymen and set patrol areas around paths.
  • Add a mechanic for inspections and breakdown fixes.
  • Use patrol zones to keep high-traffic areas consistently clean.

5) Expand Smart

  • Add a food/drink stall near rides to increase spending.
  • Build a small coaster once income is steady.
  • Keep queues shaded/short and avoid path bottlenecks.

Tip: Guest flow matters—wide paths near hubs pay off.

6) Saves & Sharing

Save any time; the browser version supports import/export so you can continue on desktop.

  • Keep a “milestone” save before major expansions.
  • Experiment with scenery themes once your finances are stable.
